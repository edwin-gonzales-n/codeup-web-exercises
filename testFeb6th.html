Latin Bites

Latin Bites, also referred to as The Peruvian Restaurant, is a classy Peruvian Cuisine restaurant in midtown Houston who serves thousands of customers. Latin Bites started 10 years ago from an Indian family who were refugees. Their attention to detail have won them their reputation, aside from their very traditional, and excellent cuisine. Latin Bites is one of my favorite restaurants because I never leave dissapointed. The spices used are fresh, the bread is exquisite, and the seasoning is just perfect. I recommend Latin Bites to anyone who would like to try some intense flavors!
